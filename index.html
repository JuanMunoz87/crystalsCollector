Crystals Collector

Instructions

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There will be four crystals displayed on the screen. When you click on a crystal, it will add a specific amount of points to the player's total score.

The crystals hiden numbers will only be exposed until you click a crystal.

You win if the total score matches the random number you were given at the beginning of the game.

You will lose if your score goes above the given random number.

The game restarts whenever you win or loses.

When the game restarts again, you should see a new random number. Also, all the crystals will have four new hidden values. Of course, your score will reset to zero.
